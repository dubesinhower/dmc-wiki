## About

Hardware company producing FPGA-based internal HDMI mods and upscalers for retro consoles.

Created the RetroGEM (universal internal HDMI mod covering PS1, PS2, N64, GameCube, Wii and more), the N64Digital, and the Morph 4K upscaler. Their GEM + Morph ecosystem is the main competitor to the RetroTINK line.
