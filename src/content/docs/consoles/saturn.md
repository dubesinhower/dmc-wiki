## Display Mods

### RGB Output

- The Sega Saturn outputs RGB natively via its A/V multi-out connector on most revisions. A SCART or BNC cable into an upscaler is the common path.
- **10-Pin DIN Breakout** (zaxour) — A breakout board for the Saturn's 10-pin DIN connector. See [GitHub](https://github.com/zaxour/Saturn_10Pin_DIN_Breakout). Community member Bonzo noted a "decadin version" available.
- HDMI mods for the Saturn have been discussed but no widely available internal solution exists as of early 2026. Community member JesusBurnsNeon linked the Saturn to a hypothetical "GEM" era solution: "if we get a SNES GEM, we get a Saturn GEM."

## Storage / Flash Carts

- Saturn game media is CD-ROM; standard flash carts are not applicable. See ODE section below.

## Common Repairs

### Known Failure Points

- **CD drive laser** — Saturn drives are aging and laser failure is common. Replacement laser units exist for the most common drive revisions.
- **CD drive belt** — Drive belt degrades; replacement is a standard repair.
- **Capacitors** — Electrolytic caps on aging boards. Audio and power sections are primary candidates.
- **Battery (backup memory)** — The onboard CR2032 battery maintains BRAM (internal saves). Replacement is a straightforward through-hole swap.

### Capacitor Replacement

- Relevant on both main board and power supply board. The Saturn has multiple board revisions (VA0, VA1, VA2, VA4, VA6, VA8, VA13+) with different layouts; confirm revision before sourcing caps.
